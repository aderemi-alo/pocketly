We collect information to provide better services to all our users. We collect information in the following ways:

### A. Information You Give Us
- **Account Information:** When you register for an account, we collect your **email address** and password. This is used to authenticate you and sync your data across devices.
- **Expense Data:** We collect the data you input into the app, including expense amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and any other financial transaction details you choose to record.

### B. Information We Collect Automatically

- **Log Data:** When you use our Service, our servers automatically record information, including your IP address, browser type, referring URLs, crash reports, and timestamp information.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
